# 人称代词的所有格用法

**人称代词的所有格写法 = 人称代词 + 名词**


| 我的     | my    |
| ------ | ----- |
| 你的/你们的 | your  |
| 他的     | his   |
| 她的     | her   |
| 它的     | its   |
| 我们的    | our   |
| 他们的    | their |

> 我的学生的高的 
> My student is tall
> your friends are short
> it is my table
> is it my table?
> yes.it is my table 
> I am your teacher 
> am I your teacher?
> NO,you aren't my teacher
> that is her house 
> is that her house?
