# L10_Every的用法

**every的用法: "every" = "每一个",属于形容词
"每一个" 算是单数,必须和单数名词配合,所以Be动词要用 `is`**

> 每一个学生都是快乐的 
> Every student is happy.
> Every house is green.
> Is every house green?
> Why is every house green?
> Every postman is busy.
> Every cat is clean 
> Is every cat clean? 
> Why is every cat clean?
